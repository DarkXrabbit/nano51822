Detailed Description

Battery Service Class.

This class implements the Battery Service with the Battery Level characteristic. During initialization it adds the Battery Service and Battery Level characteristic to the BLE stack database. Optionally it can also add a Report Reference descriptor to the Battery Level characteristic (used when including the Battery Service in the HID service).
